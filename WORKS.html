Prusıas ad Hypıum Ancient Town Visitor Center

As it is known, Düzce is an earthquake zone. There is currently a museum in the ancient city of Düzce. The museum is being prepared for demolition because the current structure is not earthquake resistant. The region needs a visitor center that can host visitors. In this project, I designed a visitor center structure by taking advantage of the topographic features of the region.
